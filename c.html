Ques.3  John went to a magic show where he saw an amazing trick, you give the magician two numbers
        X and Y and M prime numbers,the magician will tell you the amount of integers that are divisible
        by atleast one of the M primes (X and Y inclusive).You don't find it much amusing, Your task, 
        Simple,perform the trick .


	INPUT
	First line: M and  Q(the number of queries)
	Second line: M primes
	Next Q lines, containing X and Y

	OUTPUT
	Print Q lines , containing the answer of each query

	SAMPLE INPUT 
	2 1
	2 3
	1 10
	SAMPLE OUTPUT 
	7
	Explanation
	2,3,4,6,8,9,10 are the 7 numbers.
